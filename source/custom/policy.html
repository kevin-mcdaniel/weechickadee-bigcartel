Returns and Exchanges

I gladly accept returns, exchanges, and cancellations

  • Contact me within: 14 days of delivery
  • Ship items back within: 30 days of delivery
  • Request a cancellation within: 12 hours of purchase

Return and Exchange Details

I really want you to be happy with your purchase from my shop and ask that you contact me immediately if you have any problems or concerns with your order. I hope that you never want to return any item(s) that you've purchased, however if you need to here is our shop policy:

You may return any unused item(s) within 30 days of the sale for a refund or exchange. The item(s) must be in new condition (yarn in original skeins and fiber in braids). I will refund your purchase price, less shipping costs. Return shipping costs are your responsibility. If you are planning a return, please send me a message so that I can anticipate the transaction and respond to you accordingly.

I want your purchase to be a happy one, so if there is anything I can do please let me know. I'm here to help and keep my customers happy!

Questions about your order?

Please contact me if you have any problems with your order.

Payment

I like for you to receive your order quickly, so shipments are generally processed the same day or day after payment is received.

Shipping

I provide a tracking number with each shipment, however please note that the buyer assumes risk of loss or damage during shipping if insurance is not purchased.

US Orders:

I ship orders by First Class Mail through the USPS to all US customers. Packages shipped USPS First Class Mail will generally reach their destination within 3-5 business days from the date shipped. If you wish to have your orders reach you more quickly, please send me a message for other options.

International Orders:

For shipping to international addresses, I ship via USPS First Class International. This option is the least expensive option and generally reaches its destination within 12 business days. If you'd like to receive your order more quickly, please send me a message and I can provide you with other shipping options and the costs associated with those options. In addition, buyers are responsible for any duties, taxes, or fees imposed beyond the US postage that is paid at the time of purchase.
